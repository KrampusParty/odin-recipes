Mango Fish Tacos

Description

These Fish Tacos are always a success when I have visits. The tasty fried fish and sweet mango make a great pairing, making it one of my favorite recipes during the summer.

Ingredients for 2

  • 350gr of hake fillets
  • 200gr of flour
  • 50gr of cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons of Paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 2 pinchs of monosodium glutamate
  • 1 can of beer
  • 1 tablespoon and a half of mustard
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 mango
  • 1 red onion
  • cilantro
  • red pepper
  • 3 tablespoons of mayo
  • 4 tablespoons of sour cream
  • 1 lime

Instructions

  1. Cut the fish in stripes.
  2. On a bowl, mix the flour, garlic powder and monosodium glutamate.
  3. Add beer and mustard to the bowl, stir it.
  4. Dice the mango, the tomato, the onion and the pepper, mix them on another bowl, add cilantro.
  5. Mix some mayo, lime and sour cream on yet another bowl.
  6. Put the cut fish in the flour mix, fry it on a pan with abundant sunflower oil.
  7. Serve the fish and each bowl with a plate of corn tortillas, we are now ready to prepare our tacos.
