Vegan Lentils Soup

Description

There's nothing better than a hot plate of lentils during a cold winter day.

I've been eating this recipe since I dropped meat from my diet years ago and i've never missed it on my lentils ever since.

Ingredients for two servings:

  • 125 gr of lentils
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 medium sized onions
  • 1 carrot on the bigger size
  • 1/2 of green pepper
  • 1 red pepper
  • 4 tomatoes
  • Cinnamon
  • Cumin
  • Turmeric
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Leave the lentils in water the night before cooking.
  2. Cut the onions julianna style and set them aside.
  3. Mince the garlic cloves, cook them slowly, when they start to dance a little, add the onion and salt. Mix well.
  4. Turn up the heat, stir them nicely for a while, turn down the fire. Let the onion caramelize on its own.
  5. Dice the carrots, the tomatoes (take the seeds out) and the peppers, set them aside separately.
  6. Add the carrots to the pot, slowly cook them for 5 minutes, add the peppers, add some water and let it boil for 15 minutes, stir from time to time.
  7. Add the tomatoes, spices and the lentils. Cover the pot and let it boil till the lentills are done.
